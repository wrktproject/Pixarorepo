## Algorithm References

Our image processing is inspired by professional photo editing software and implements:
- sRGB gamma correction (IEC 61966-2-1 standard)
- Perceptually uniform color adjustments
- Luminance-based tone mapping
- Bilateral filtering for noise reduction
- Gaussian blur for clarity effects
